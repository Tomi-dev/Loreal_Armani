FAQ

  • HOW CAN I BOOK MY MASTERCLASS? Click on BOOK NOW, follow the steps and select your masterclass. You will receive a confirmation email, with your booking details and the TEAM MEETING link to access the live masterclass
  • CAN I BOOK MORE THAN ONE MASTERCLASS? No, each printed invitation is valid to book only one masterclass. The invitation is strictly personal and can’t be transferred
  • HOW DOES A TEAM MEETING WORK? Click on the link that you will receive by email to join the masterclass to be taken to a page where you can choose to either join on the web or download the desktop app. If you already have the Teams app, the meeting will open there automatically. If you don’t have a Teams account, you have the option to enter your name to join the meeting as a guest. If you do have a Teams account, select Sign in to join.
  • WHAT IS THE MASTERCLASS FORMAT? During the first 30 minutes the face designer will show you how to recreate a look on a model, explaining each step and sharing secret tips. In the meantime, you will have the chance to ask questions or comment in the chat. During the last 15 minutes the face designers will be at your full disposal to answer your additional questions or requests and, if you would like, you can activate your camera and microphone to interact more.
  • IS IT POSSIBLE TO ASK QUESTIONS TO THE FACE DESIGNERS? Of course! During the first 30 you can ask questions or comment in the chat, meanwhile the last 15 minutes will be dedicated to answering all of your questions.
  • SHOULD I PUT MY CAMERA ON? Up to you: the last 15 minutes of the masterclass will be your moment to interact with the face designers and if you wish you can turn on the camera and share the look you created.
  • I DON’T HAVE ALL THE REQUIRED MATERIALS. CAN I STILL PARTICIPATE IN THE CLASS? Yes, you can try to replicate the make-up look with other materials, or you can just listen to the masterclass and recreate the look another time.
  • IS THE BEAUTY MASTERCLASS RECORDED? No, the event is not recorded, and it will be only available in live in the Team Meeting.
  • I HAVE BOOKED A CLASS, BUT I CAN’T PARTICIPATE. WHAT SHOULD I DO? For any modification or cancellation of your initial booking, please send an email to xxx@armanibeauty.com and we will try to find you an alternative slot.
